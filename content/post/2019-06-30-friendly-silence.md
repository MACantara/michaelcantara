Friendly Silence\
A nice friend to have\
Yet difficult to please.\
Always beside me

but always silent.\
Cheering on great days\
With silence of joy\
Helping through my life,

A friend that stays.\
I wonder the days of joy
Of my little friend,\
I wonder my life\
Without you.
